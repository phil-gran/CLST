  Central Luzon School of Theology (CLST) is God's instrument in developing great and faithful leaders to grow our spirit and soul. These courses aim to equip students to effectively administer their ministry as pastors, associate pastors, church leaders, missionaries, evangelists, and teachers. They seek to provide students with general knowledge about the Christian faith. The curriculum focuses on the foundations of Biblical studies, historical-theological studies, and practical ministries.
